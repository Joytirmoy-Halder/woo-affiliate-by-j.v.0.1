# 🚀 WooCommerce Affiliate System by J

**Turn every loyal customer into a brand ambassador.**

This plugin is a complete, easy-to-use affiliate system built specifically for your WooCommerce store. It allows your members to share your products with their friends and followers and earn money (commissions) for every sale they bring in.

---

## 🌟 What this plugin does for you

### For You (The Store Owner)
*   **Boost Sales**: Get more traffic as people share your products across social media.
*   **Full Control**: You decide how much people earn (e.g., 10% of the sale or a flat $5 per order).
*   **Easy Management**: Approve or reject earnings with a single click from your WordPress dashboard.
*   **Fraud Protection**: Smart security stops people from using their own links to buy products for themselves.

### For Your Members (The Affiliates)
*   **Automatic Enrollment**: Every logged-in user becomes an affiliate instantly—no extra forms to fill out.
*   **Easy Sharing**: A "Your Affiliate Link" box appears on every product page so they can copy and share it in one click.
*   **Personal Dashboard**: A clear dashboard inside their "My Account" area showing exactly how much they've earned and who has clicked their links.

---

## 🛠 How it works (Simple Steps)

1.  **A Member Shares a Link**: A logged-in user copies their unique link for any product.
2.  **A Friend Clicks**: When a friend clicks that link, the system "remembers" them for 30 days (this is called a Cookie).
3.  **A Sale Happens**: If the friend buys anything, the system automatically calculates the commission.
4.  **You Approve**: You see the referral in your dashboard. Once you're happy the order is legitimate, you mark it as "Approved."
5.  **You Pay**: After you pay the user (via PayPal, Bank, etc.), you mark it as "Paid" to keep your records crystal clear.

---

## 📦 Simple Installation Guide

1.  **Upload**: Upload the `woo-affiliate-by-j` folder to your website's plugins folder.
2.  **Activate**: Turn it on in your WordPress "Plugins" list.
3.  **Refresh**: Go to **Settings > Permalinks** and just click **Save Changes** (this makes sure the new dashboard page works).
4.  **Set Your Rates**: Go to **WooCommerce > Affiliate Settings** and choose how much you want to pay your affiliates.
