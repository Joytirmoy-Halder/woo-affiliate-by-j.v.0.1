# WooCommerce Affiliate System by J

Welcome to your new affiliate system! This plugin is designed to be simple, lightweight, and easy to use even if you are not a technical person.

With this plugin, you can easily track who is sending customers to your store and manage their commission payments without any headache.

---

## Key Features (Made Simple)

*   Automated Tracking: The plugin automatically watches for referrals. When someone buys through an affiliate link, the commission is recorded instantly.
*   *   "Mark Paid" Button: No more manual spreadsheet tracking! Once you pay an affiliate, just click the "Mark Paid" button in your dashboard to keep your records clean.
    *   *   Clear History for Affiliates: Your affiliates get their own history table. They can see exactly which orders they earned from, the amount, and the current status (Pending, Approved, or Paid).
        *   *   Easy Search: Looking for a specific order or affiliate? Use the search bar in the admin area to find them by name, email, or Order ID.
            *   *   At-a-Glance Stats: See exactly how much you owe and how much you have already paid with simple stat cards on your dashboard.
                *   *   Robust Settings: We have optimized the settings page to be robust and error-free, so you can configure it once and let it run.
                 
                    *   ---
                 
                    *   ## How to Install (3 Easy Steps)
                 
                    *   1.  Upload: Copy the woo-affiliate-by-j folder into your website's /wp-content/plugins/ folder.
                        2.  2.  Activate: Go to your WordPress Plugins menu and click Activate on "WooCommerce Affiliate System by J".
                            3.  3.  Configure: Go to WooCommerce > Affiliate System to set your commission rates and you are ready to go!
                              
                                4.  ---
                              
                                5.  ## How to Use
                              
                                6.  ### For Admins (Store Owners)
                                7.  *   Manage all commissions under WooCommerce > Affiliate Commissions.
                                    *   *   When you pay an affiliate (e.g., via PayPal or Bank Transfer), click the green "Mark Paid" button next to their commission. This moves it to the "Processed" list.
                                     
                                        *   ### For Affiliates (Partners)
                                        *   *   Affiliates can visit their Dashboard to see a full history of their referrals.
                                            *   *   They can track their Approved (Unpaid) balance and their Total Paid history in real-time.
